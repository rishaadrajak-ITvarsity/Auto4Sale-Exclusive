Description

This 1967 Goggo 250TS coupe appears in solid condition.

This 1967 Goggo 250 TS Coupé comes with traceable history and even shows the new car invoice from back then and the first “Pappbrief”. It has been registered to the last 4th owner, who drove this Goggo regularly to weddings, etc. Today TÜV has expired, but it should pass the test with no big trouble. Electrical parts work, the gearbox shifts, and the brakes work fine. There are slight traces of oil sweat but no leakage. The interieur seats had been reupholstered once and the dashboard shows some little cracks and usage. The engine starts right up and runs reliable, although the battery needed a little kick-start. The floor shows no rust through, although light rust is visible on the chassis. This cute little car is still a reliable driver as back than, especially in those days where parking lots are rare downtown.
